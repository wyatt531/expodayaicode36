About the game

Turtle Reef is a cozy exploration game set in a hand-crafted underwater world. You’ll guide a young turtle through coral reefs, seagrass meadows, and mysterious deep-sea trenches, learning how delicate ocean ecosystems really are.

This homepage is your coral reef hub—perfect as the main menu or landing screen before players dive into the full adventure.
